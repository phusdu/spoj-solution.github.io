### **Statement**

You have two integers L and R , and you are required to find the max xor
value of a and b where L <= a <= R and L <= b <= R

### Input



Two integers in a line. L, R <= 1e9



### Output

One integer, the answer

### Example

    
    
    Input:
    1 10
    
    Output:
    15
